Questions, answered

What is a Customer Growth Agent?

It's a single AI agent that owns the whole customer lifecycle instead of one stage. You give aiRA an outcome — more signups, higher repeat rate, less churn — and it plans, launches, and optimizes the agents to get there, grounded in your data and your guardrails.

How is this different from a campaign tool or CDP?

Campaign tools wait for a human to define each campaign. A CDP organizes data but doesn't act. aiRA decides what to run: it segments, drafts, launches, and tunes a fleet of always-on, self-optimizing agents — each running its own continuous engagement loop — then reports back on every one.

What is an agent?

An agent is an always-on AI worker aiRA runs for one narrow segment — with its own audience, offer, channel, and timing. Instead of one campaign for "lapsed customers," aiRA runs a fleet of always-on agents, each working its own slice around the clock and self-optimizing every run.

Do I stay in control?

Yes. You set budget caps, channel rules, and approval gates. High-risk moves wait for sign-off, every agent is reviewable, and you can pause or edit anything aiRA proposes before it ships.

How long does setup take?

Connect your customer data and channels — most teams are live in a day. aiRA reads your existing segments and history, so it has useful context from the first prompt.
