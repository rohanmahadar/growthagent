How an agent is born

From prompt to live, always-on agents

01

Slice

aiRA splits the goal's audience into narrow segments by value, recency, and behavior.

02

Match

Each slice gets the offer, channel, and message most likely to move it.

03

Spawn

Agents launch and schedule themselves, then run continuously within your rules — self-optimizing every cycle.

04

Tune

Live results feed back; aiRA reallocates and rewrites weak agents.

05

Retire

Spent or underperforming agents auto-pause so budget follows what works.
